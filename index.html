GAME RULES AND DETAILS:

Football (soccer)-

  • Teams consist of 11 players each.
  • The objective is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opposing team's goal.

Tennis-

  • Matches can be played between two or four players.
  • The objective is to hit the ball over the net and into the opponent's court, with the aim of making it difficult for the opponent to return the ball.

Capture the Flag-

  • Teams are divided into two and each team has a flag in their territory.
  • The objective is to capture the other team's flag and bring it back to your own territory without getting tagged by an opponent.

Dodgeball-

  • Teams consist of an equal number of players on each side.
  • The objective is to eliminate all the players on the opposing team by hitting them with a ball and making it difficult for them to catch the ball.

Ultimate Frisbee-

  • Teams consist of 5, 7, or more players on each side.
  • The objective is to score points by catching the frisbee in the opposing team's end zone while passing it to teammates.

Golf-

  • Golf is played individually or in teams.
  • The objective is to hit the ball into the hole in as few strokes as possible, using a variety of clubs to navigate the course.
